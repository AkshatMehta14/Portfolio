Animals

Animals teach empathy and responsibility — they inspire my work and values.

Caring for my pet chicken Buffy sparked my interest in humane technology and ethics. I’ve led community efforts with shelters and advocacy to improve animal welfare.

I co-authored a paper introducing AnimalHarmBench (AHB), a benchmark for risks of animal harm in LLM-generated text. AHB includes 1,850 curated Reddit questions and 2,500 synthetic questions across 50 animal categories and 50 ethical scenarios, evaluated with an LLM-as-a-judge framework and debiasing. We find significant differences across models, categories, and scenarios, and outline future directions for technical research.
